**lat** -- estimated latitude
-----------------------------

This attribute is the geographic latitude.
Locations north of the equator have positive latitudes.

* **Field width:** 9
* **Format:** %9.4f
* **Null:** -999.0000
* **Units:** Degrees
* **Range:** lat >= -90.0 && lat <= 90.0
