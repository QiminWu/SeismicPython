ema – emergence angle¶

This attribute is the emergence angle of an arrival, as observed at a three-component station or array. The value increases from the vertical direction towards the horizontal.

  • Field width: 7
  • Format: %7.2f
  • Null: -1.00
  • Units: Degrees
  • Range: ema >= 0.0 && ema <= 90.0
