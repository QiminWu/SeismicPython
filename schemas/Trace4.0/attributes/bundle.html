bundle – database pointer to bundled/grouped data¶

Abundle is a special database pointer which refers to either an entire table or view, or to a contiguous subset of some table or view. In the former case, both the record number and the field number should be dbALL. In the latter case, the record number and the field number specify a range of records in the table.

  • Field width: 32
  • Format: %ld %ld %ld %ld
