edepth – emplacement depth¶

This attribute gives the depth at which the instrument is positioned, relative to the value of elev in the site relation.

  • Field width: 9
  • Format: %9.4f
  • Null: 0.0000
  • Units: Kilometers
  • Range: edepth >= 0.0
