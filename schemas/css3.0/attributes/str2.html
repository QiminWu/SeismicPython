str2 – Strike of second nodal plane¶

Strike of Second Nodal Plane; In degrees (for sign conventions see Aki and Richards, 1980, p. 106). In fplane, as determied. In moment, refers to best-fitting double couple (see bestdc).

  • Field width: 5
  • Format: %5.1f
  • Null: 999.9
