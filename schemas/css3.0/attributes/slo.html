slo – slowness (s/km)¶

This is the observed slowness of a wave as it sweeps across an array or the slowness used to compute a beam.

  • Field width: 7
  • Format: %7.4f
  • Null: -1.0000
  • Units: Seconds/km
  • Range: slo >= 0.0
