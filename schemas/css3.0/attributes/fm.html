fm – first motion¶

This is a two-character indication of first motion. The first character describes first motion seen on short-period channels and the second holds for long-period instruments. Compression (dilation) on a short-period sensor is denoted by c(d) and compression (dilation) on a long-period sensor is denoted by u(r). Empty character positions will be indicated by dots (e.g., ‘.r’). Empty character positions indicate a measurement that is not determinable or applicable.

  • Field width: 2
  • Format: %-2s
  • Null: -
  • Range: fm =~ /[cd.][ur.]/
