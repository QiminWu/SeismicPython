sdobs – standard error of observation¶

This attribute is derived from the discrepancies in the arrival times of the phases used to locate an event. It is defined as the square root of the sum of the squares of the time residuals, divided by the number of degrees of freedom. The latter is the number of defining observations (ndef in origin) minus the dimension of the system solved (4 if depth is allowed to be a free variable, 3 if depth is constrained).

  • Field width: 9
  • Format: %9.4f
  • Null: -1.0000
  • Range: sdobs > 0.0
