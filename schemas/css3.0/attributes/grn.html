grn – geographic region number¶

This is a geographic region number, as defined by Flinn, Engdahl and Hill (Bull. Seism. Soc. Amer. vol 64, pp. 771-992, 1974). See grname.

  • Field width: 8
  • Format: %8ld
  • Null: -1
  • Range: grn > 0
