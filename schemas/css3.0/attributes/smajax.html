smajax – semi-major axis of error¶

This is the length of the semi-major axis of the location error ellipse. It is found by projecting the covariance matrix onto the horizontal plane. The level of confidence is specified by conf. See sdepth, sminax and stx.

  • Field width: 9
  • Format: %9.4f
  • Null: -1.0000
  • Units: Kilometers
  • Range: smajax > 0.0
