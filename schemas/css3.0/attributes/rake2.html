rake2 – Rake of Second Nodal Plane¶

Slip of First and Second Nodal Planes; In degrees (for sign conventions see Aki and Richards, 1980, p. 106). In fplane, as determied. In moment, refers to best-fitting double couple (see bestdc).

  • Field width: 6
  • Format: %6.1f
  • Null: 9999.9
