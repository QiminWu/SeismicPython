lon – estimated longitude¶

This attribute is the geographic longitude in degrees. Longitudes are measured positive east of the Greenwich meridian.

  • Field width: 9
  • Format: %9.4f
  • Null: -999.0000
  • Units: Degrees
  • Range: lon >= -180.0 && lon <= 180.0
