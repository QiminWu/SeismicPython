demean – Time-series was was demeaned¶

In many situations it is appropriate to remove the mean value before calculating the spectrum. This flag indicates whether the mean was removed in the time-series (‘y’) or not (‘n’) ;

  • Field width: 1
  • Format: %-1s
  • Null: -
  • Range: demean =~ /y|n/
