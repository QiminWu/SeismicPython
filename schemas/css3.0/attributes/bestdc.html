bestdc – Moment of best-fitting double couple¶

Best Double Couple; If the moment tensor T and P eigenvalues are s1 and s2, respectively, then the scalar siesmic moment is defined as bestdc = (s1+s2)/2. Given in units of mexpon (which see).

  • Field width: 5
  • Format: %5.2f
