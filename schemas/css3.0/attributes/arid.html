arid – arrival id¶

Each arrival is assigned a unique positive integer identifying it with a unique sta, chan and time. This number is used in the assoc relation along with the origin identifier to link arrival and origin.

  • Field width: 8
  • Format: %8ld
  • Null: -1
  • Range: arid > 0
