paxplg – Plunge of P-axis vector¶

Compression Axis Azimuth and Plunge; In fplane, as determined. In moment, obtained by rotation of the largestt positve eigenvalue. In degrees, for conventions see Aki & Richards (1980).

  • Field width: 5
  • Format: %5.1f
  • Null: 999.9
