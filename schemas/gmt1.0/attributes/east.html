east – easternmost longitude of file¶

This field records the eastern longitude boundary of the GMT file. The phase may be unwrapped, in which case longitudes of -180 to 0 may appear as 180 to 360 degrees.

  • Field width: 9
  • Format: %9.4lf
  • Null: -999.0000
  • Units: Degrees
  • Range: east >= -180 && east <= 360
