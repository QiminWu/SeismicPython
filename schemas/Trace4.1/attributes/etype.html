etype – event type¶

This attribute is used to identify the type of seismic event, when known. For etypes l, r, t the value in origin will be the value determined by the station closest to the event.

  • Field width: 7
  • Format: %-7s
  • Null: -
  • Range: etype =~ /qb|eq|me|ex|o|l|r|t/
