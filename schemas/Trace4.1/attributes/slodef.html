slodef – slowness = defining, non-defining¶

This one-character flag indicates whether or not the slowness of a phase is d (defining), or n (non-defining) for the origin associated with this arrival. See azdef and timedef.

  • Field width: 1
  • Format: %-1s
  • Null: -
  • Range: slodef =~ /d|n/
