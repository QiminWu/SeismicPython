vang – vertical angle¶

This attribute measures the angle between the sensitive axis of a seismometer and the outward-pointing vertical direction. For a vertically oriented seismometer, vang = 0. For a horizontally oriented seismometer, vang =90. See hang.

  • Field width: 6
  • Format: %6.1f
  • Null: 0.0
  • Units: Degrees
  • Range: vang >= 0.0 && vang <= 90.0
