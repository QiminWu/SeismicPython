calibration – Station-Channel calibration parameters¶

This table provides a record of updates in the calibration of a station-channel for a specific time interval are provided here. There is one entry for each calibration period. Calib, calper, and units are given for the complete system response.

Fields¶

calib calper chan dlchan dlname dlsta
endtime fc insname lddate lead samprate
segtype snname sta stream time units

Primary Keys¶
