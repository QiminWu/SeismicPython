rsptype – response type¶

Originally, this field characterized the response information specified by the neighboring attribute dir and dfile. However, in Antelope datasets, it is used to indicate the “natural” units for the instrument – ie, ‘A’ (acceleration), ‘V’ (velocity), ‘D’ (displacement) ‘I’ (infrasound), or ‘H’ (hydroacoustic).

  • Field width: 6
  • Format: %-6s
  • Null: -
