review – review status¶

This indicates the level of review for an origin. A value of “y” indicates the origin has been inspected. Higher levels of review indicate a higher degree of confidence in the location. Typical levels might be “auto” or null for automatic (no human review) locations, “pre”liminary and “fin”al.

  • Field width: 4
  • Format: %-4s
  • Null: -
