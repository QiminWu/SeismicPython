staname – station description¶

This is the full name of the station whose code-name is in sta. As an example, one record in the site relation connects sta = ANMO to staname = ALBUQUERQUE, NEW MEXICO (SRO).

  • Field width: 50
  • Format: %-50s
  • Null: -
