commid – comment id¶

This is a key used to point to free-form comments entered in the remark relation. These comments store additional information about a tuple in another relation. Within the remark relation, there may be many tuples with the same commid and different lineno, but the same commid will appear in only one other tuple among the rest of the relations in the database. See lineno.

  • Field width: 12
  • Format: %12ld
  • Null: -1
  • Range: commid > 0
