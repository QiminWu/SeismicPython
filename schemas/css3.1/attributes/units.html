units – units of calib (e.g. nm, nm/sec, ...)¶

This is the units which the calibration are measured. Normally the units are nm for displacement data, nm/sec for velocity data, nm/sec**2 for acceleration data.

  • Field width: 12
  • Format: %-12s
  • Null: -
