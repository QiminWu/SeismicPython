mechid – mechanism id¶

Each focal mechanism needs to assigned a unique mechanism id. A seperate key from orid is necessary because it is possible for different methods to be used to produce different solutions from the same first motion data. Note this key is for traditional focal mechanism solutions only. The moment table is used for moment tensor solutions and each moment tensor solution will have a unique orid.

  • Field width: 12
  • Format: %12ld
  • Null: -1
  • Range: mechid > 0
