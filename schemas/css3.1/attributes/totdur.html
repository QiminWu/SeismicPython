totdur – Total Duration of data used for spectra¶

This parameter is relevant for Welsh averaged spectra . For multitaper spectral estimates would set this value to twin or null.

  • Field width: 12
  • Format: %12.2f
  • Null: -1.00
  • Range: totdur > 0.0
