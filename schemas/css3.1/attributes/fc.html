fc – center frequency¶

This is a frequency in Hertz that is in the center of a particular pass band. This is used to describe the center frequency of a seismic instrument pass band, for instance.

  • Field width: 11
  • Format: %11.6f
  • Null: -1.000000
  • Range: fc >= 0.0
