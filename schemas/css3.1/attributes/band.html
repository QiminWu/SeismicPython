band – frequency band¶

This is a qualitative indicator of frequency pass-band for an instrument. Values should reflect the response curve rather than just the sample rate. Recommended values are s (short-period), m (mid-period), i (intermediate-period), l (long-period), b (broad-band), h (high frequency, very short-period), and v (very long-period). For a better notion of the instrument characteristics, see the instrument response curve.

  • Field width: 1
  • Format: %-1s
  • Null: -
  • Range: band =~ /s|m|i|l|b|h|v/
