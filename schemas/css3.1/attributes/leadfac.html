leadfac – leading factor¶

This factor is used to correct the situation of using a 24-bit A/D converter and storing the data in a 4 byte word. If the data are stored in the lower 3 bytes of the 4 byte word then leadfac=1.0 If the data are stored in the high bytes then it will be a large factor which is divided from gnom.

  • Field width: 11
  • Format: %11.7f
  • Null: 0.0000000
  • Range: leadfac > 0.0
