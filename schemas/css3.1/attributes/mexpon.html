mexpon – Exponent of values below¶

Exponet of Moment Scale Factor; The power of 10 by which scalar moment (bestdc) or moment tensor components (mrr, mtt, etc.) and their corresponding errors are to be multiplied to give their actual values. As an example, if mexpon=17 and bestdc=1.3, then the scalar moment is given by 1.3x10**17 N.-m.

  • Field width: 3
  • Format: %3ld
