naxplg – Plunge of N-axis vector¶

Null Axis Azimuth and Plunge; Obtained by rotation of the moment tensor into the principal axes system. Associated with the small intermediate eigen value. In degrees, for conventions see Aki & Richards (1980).

  • Field width: 5
  • Format: %5.1f
  • Null: 999.9
