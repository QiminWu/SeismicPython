**predaz** -- predicted azimuth
-------------------------------

This is the predicted station-to-event
azimuth measured clockwise from north. Azimuth is predicted
from the results of a location algorithm.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.2f
* **Null:** -1.00
* **Units:** Degrees
* **Range:** predaz >= 0.0 && predaz < 360.0
