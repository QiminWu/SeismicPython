**sdepth** -- depth error
-------------------------

This is the maximum error of a depth estimate
for a level of confidence given by conf. See smajax,
sminax, stx.

* **Field width:** 9
* **Format:** %9.4f
* **Null:** -1.0000
* **Units:** Kilometers
* **Range:** sdepth > 0.0
