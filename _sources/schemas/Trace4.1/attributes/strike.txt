**strike** -- strike of the semi-major axis
-------------------------------------------

This attribute is
the strike of the semi-major axis of the location error
ellipse, measured in degrees clockwise from North. See
smajax.

* **Field width:** 6
* **Format:** %6.2f
* **Null:** -1.00
* **Units:** Degrees
* **Range:** strike >= 0.0 && strike < 360.0
