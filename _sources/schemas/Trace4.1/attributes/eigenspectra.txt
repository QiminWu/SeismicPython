**eigenspectra** -- eigenspectra
--------------------------------

pointer to the array of fft coefficients for all tapers, ie
the nth coefficient for taper j is
\*(eigenspectra + j \* nfft + n),  c
or
eigenspectra(n,j)    FORTRAN

* **Field width:** 20
* **Format:** %20ld
