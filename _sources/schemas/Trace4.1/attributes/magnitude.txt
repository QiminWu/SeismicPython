**magnitude** -- magnitude
--------------------------

This gives the magnitude value of the type
indicated in attribute magtype. It is derived in a variety
of ways, which are not necessarily linked directly to an
arrival. See imb, iml, ims, magtype, mb, ml and ms.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.2f
* **Null:** 0.00
