.. _css3.0-amp_attributes:

**amp** -- amplitude, instrument corrected, nm
----------------------------------------------

This is the zero-to-peak amplitude of the earth's
displacement for a seismic phase.  Amp is assumed to be
corrected for the response of the instrument.

* **Field width:** 10
* **Format:** %10.1f
* **Null:** -1.0
* **Units:** Nanometers
* **Range:** amp > 0.0
