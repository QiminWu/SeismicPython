.. _css3.0-edepth_attributes:

**edepth** -- emplacement depth
-------------------------------

This attribute gives the depth in kilometers at which
the instrument is positioned, relative to the value of
elev in the site relation.

* **Field width:** 9
* **Format:** %9.4f
* **Null:** -9.9999
* **Units:** kilometers
* **Range:** edepth >= 0.0
