**taxplg** -- Plunge of T-axis
------------------------------

Tension Azis Azimuth and Plunge; In fplane, as determined.
In moment, obtained by rotation of the moment tensor into
the principal axes system.  Associated with the largest
(in the absolute sense) negative eigenvalue.  In degrees,
for conventions see Aki & Richards (1980).

* **Field width:** 5
* **Format:** %5.1f
* **Null:** 999.9
