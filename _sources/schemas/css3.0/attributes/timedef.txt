**timedef** -- time = defining, non-defining
--------------------------------------------

This one character flag indicates whether the time of a
phase is d (defining), or n (non-defining) for this
arrival.  See azdef and slodef.

* **Field width:** 1
* **Format:** %-1s
* **Null:** -
* **Range:** timedef =~ /d|n/
