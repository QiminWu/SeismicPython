**mbid** -- mb magid
--------------------

This stores the magid for a record in netmag.  Mbid is a
foreign key joining origin to netmag where origin.  mbid =
netmag.  magid.  See magid, mlid and msid.

* **Field width:** 8
* **Format:** %8ld
* **Null:** -1
* **Range:** mbid > 0
