**gcalib** -- correction to scale factor
----------------------------------------

This is the correction to the nominal scaling factor of
this stage.  gnom multiplied by gcalib yields the exact
scale factor for the stage.  gcalib is dimensionless.

* **Field width:** 10
* **Format:** %10.6f
* **Null:** 0.000000
* **Range:** gcalib > 0.0
