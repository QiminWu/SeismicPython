**dip2** -- Dip of second nodal plane
-------------------------------------

Dip of second Nodal Plane; In degrees (for sign
conventions see Aki and Richards, 1980, p.  106).  In
fplane, as determied.  In moment, refers to best-fitting
double couple (see bestdc).

* **Field width:** 5
* **Format:** %5.1f
* **Null:** 999.9
