.. _css3.1-drtime_attributes:

**drtime** -- derived event time
--------------------------------

Derived depth; Corresponds to the
derived-eventtime parameter in the USGS/NEIC GeoJSON
data feed for moment tensors.

* **Field width:** 18
* **Format:** %18.6f
* **Null:** -9999999999.999000
* **Units:** Epochal seconds
