**tmrr** -- mrr moment tensor component
---------------------------------------

Moment Tensor Component; Corresponds to the
tensor-mrr parameter in the USGS/NEIC GeoJSON
data feed for moment tensors. According to obspy
documentation the r, t and p axes are positive
up, north and west.

* **Field width:** 12
* **Format:** %12.5e
* **Null:** -9.99999e+99
