.. _css3.1-izero_attributes:

**izero** -- index of FIR coeff. for 0th sample
-----------------------------------------------

Index within fir coefficients corresponding to the 0th
sample.  This is needed for asymetric FIR filters, or
filters that are causal.

* **Field width:** 8
* **Format:** %8ld
* **Null:** 0
* **Range:** izero >= 0
