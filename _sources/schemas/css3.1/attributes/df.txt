**df** -- Frequency interval (Hertz) in spectral file
-----------------------------------------------------

We assume all spectra are discretized at equal frequency
intervals (unless spectra are stored in fap2 files).
df is the frequency interval.

* **Field width:** 15
* **Format:** %15.6lg
* **Null:** -1
* **Units:** Hertz
* **Range:** df > 0.0
