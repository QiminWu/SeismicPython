.. _css3.1-esaz_attributes:

**esaz** -- event to station azimuth
------------------------------------

This attribute is the calculated event-to-station azimuth,
measured in degrees clockwise from North.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.2f
* **Null:** -999.00
* **Units:** Degrees
* **Range:** esaz >= 0.0 && esaz <= 360.0
