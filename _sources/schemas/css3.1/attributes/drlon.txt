**drlon** -- derived event longitude
------------------------------------

Derived event longitude; Corresponds to the
derived-longitude parameter in the USGS/NEIC GeoJSON
data feed for moment tensors.

* **Field width:** 12
* **Format:** %12.7f
* **Null:** -999.0000
* **Units:** Degrees
* **Range:** lon >= -180.0 && lon <= 180.0
