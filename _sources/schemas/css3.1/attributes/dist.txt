**dist** -- estimated distance
------------------------------

This attribute gives the approximate source-receiver
distance as calculated from slowness (array measurements
only), incident angle, or (S-P) times.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.2f
* **Null:** -1.00
* **Units:** Degrees
* **Range:** dist >= 0.0 && dist <= 180.0
