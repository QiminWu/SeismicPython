**dlname** -- dbbuild datalogger selection name
-----------------------------------------------

In the calibration table, this is the datalogger
selected within dbbuild.  In theory, together with
snname this allows deriving dbbuild input from a
database.  dlname has other meanings in other tables.

* **Field width:** 32
* **Format:** %s
* **Null:** -
