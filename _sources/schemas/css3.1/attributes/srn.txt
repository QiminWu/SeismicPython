**srn** -- seismic region number
--------------------------------

This is a seismic region number, as given by Flinn,
Engdahl and Hill (Bull.  Seism.  Soc.  Amer.  vol 64, pp
791-992, 1974).  See grn, grname and srname.

* **Field width:** 8
* **Format:** %8ld
* **Null:** -1
* **Range:** srn > 0
