**semax** -- maximum east-west slowness (s/km)
----------------------------------------------

This is a maximum east-west slowness range.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.4f
* **Null:** -9.9999
* **Units:** Seconds/km
* **Range:** semax > -9.9999 && semax <= 9.9999
