**semin** -- minimum east-west slowness (s/km)
----------------------------------------------

This is a minimum east-west slowness range.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.4f
* **Null:** -9.9999
* **Units:** Seconds/km
* **Range:** semin > -9.9999 && semin <= 9.9999
