.. _css3.1-slowd_attributes:

**slowd** -- observed slowness width (s/km)
-------------------------------------------

This is the observed slowness width of the peak lobe in a
slowness grid.

* **Field width:** 7
* **Format:** %7.4f
* **Null:** -1.0000
* **Units:** Seconds/km
* **Range:** slowd >= 0.0
